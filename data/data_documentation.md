Investment in Private Nonresidential Fixed Assets by Industry, Asset Type, and Year  
-> file name: "detailnonres_inv1.xlsx"  
-> time period: 1901 - 2022, yearly  
-> source: Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA)  
-> obtained from: https://apps.bea.gov/national/FA2004/Details/Index.htm  
-> accessed on: August 15, 2024, redownloaded December 13, 2024  
-> instructions: Data is obtained from the BEA Detailed Data for Fixed Assets and Consumer Durable Goods (Detailed estimates by industry and by type of asset). To access, navigate to the link above and download the "Investment" spreadsheet under "Nonresidential Detailed Estimates".  
-> cleaning process: We aggregate across industries to calculate total investment by year and asset type.
